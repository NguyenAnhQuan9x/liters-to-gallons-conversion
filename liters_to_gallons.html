Liters to Gallons Conversion

There are two popular volume units: gallons and liters. The gallon is the English measure of volume, while the liter is the metric unit. The gallon in America and the gallon in Britain, however, are not the same! The actual measurement of a gallon in the US is 231 cubic inches, or 3.785411784 liters. The UK gallon or Imperial gallon is equivalent to about 277.42 cubic inches. Make sure you understand which country is being asked for if you're requested to perform the conversion; otherwise, you won't get the right response. Although the American gallon is used in this example, the setup for the issue is the same for the Imperial gallon (just using 277.42 instead of 3.785).

Liters to Gallons Formula

Although it isn't quite as simple as converting between metric units, which are just multiples of 10, it is still not a difficult process.
The conversion factor is:
1 liter = 0.264 US gallons
To find how many gallons are in 4 liters, for example:
gallons = 4 liters x 0.264 gallons/liter
The liters cancel out, leaving the gallon unit:
4 liters = 1.056 gallons

Keep this in mind: there are about 4 liters per US gallon. So, a half gallon is about 2 liters, while 2 gallons is around 8 liters.
